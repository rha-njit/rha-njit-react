Transportation, Facilities, and Security

  • Transportation
  • Facilities
  • Security
  • Flying

    For those wishing to fly, Newark International Airport is the closest airport to NJIT. From the airport to campus, it is less than a 15 minute drive via I-21 N. Our drivers will be providing transportation between the airport, hotel, and NJIT throughout the conference.

  • Driving

    For those that intend to drive, to following address may be used to direct you to conference check-in: Best Western Plus Robert Treat Hotel, 50 Park Pl, Newark, NJ 07102.

  • Facilities

    NJIT is an ideal place for a conference like RBC because it offers a small, homely atmosphere for the conference. The venues for this conference will include the Campus Center, Central King Building, Tiernan Hall, and Cullimore Hall. All of these spaces are equipped with Wi-Fi, charging stations, and any necessary equipment for presentations.

  • Security

    Police officers are on duty 24/7, patrolling in police cruisers, dispatching and providing information at the headquarters, and being posted at various buildings and areas around campus. They are armed and have full powers of arrest throughout New Jersey. In addition to their standard patrol routes, they provide escort and transport services to those who request it. For our conference, we will have a hired professional to ensure the safety of our delegates. NJIT also has a blue light system throughout campus at optimal locations.
