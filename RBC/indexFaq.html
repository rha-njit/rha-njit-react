  • Are there any additional costs to park at the hotel?

    Yes, $20 per night

  • When will my institution receive our invoice?

    An invoice will be generated and sent to the email listed under the "Contact Information" section of the registration form.

  • Is there an extra fee with those using a credit card to pay for registration?

    No, there will be no extra charge for using a credit card.

  • How can I connect to NJIT Wifi?

    The "NJITguest" wireless network is intended for outside guests and alumni visting the NJIT campus. This network is similar to the way you get wireless at your local coffee shop. It is open, unencrypted (password exchange during authentication is encrypted). It provides public Internet their access and access to NJIT's public Internet resources. Guests can creat their own account through a self-service portal for 24-hour access. Credentials are sent via email or SMS (cell phone) text message.

  • Where will housing be?

    Best Western Plus Robert Treat Hotel, located in downtown Newark.
