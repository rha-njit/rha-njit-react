    The Regional Business Conference team at the New Jersey Institute of Technology is super excited to host the CAACURH 2020 Regional Business Conference! We are looking forward to meeting you all in person and having a fun weekend. Below is some information for registering for the conference:


    Registration Timeline

    Registration begins: December 9, 2019
    Registration closes: January 3, 2020
    Late registration opens: January 4, 2020
    Late registration closes: January 10, 2020

    *As a note, an additional $10 fee will be added for each delegate that registers late.*


    Registration Costs

    Delegate: $120
    Advisor Double: $178
    Advisor Single: $263


    Delegate Cap

    The conference is currently capped at four delegates and one advisor per school.


    Confirmation Email

    After each delegation has registered, a confirmation email and invoice will be sent to the email listed on the "Contact Information" section of the registration form from the respective institution.


    Check In

    Check in will start at 3 p.m. on Friday, February 21, 2020.
